# Bill of Materials (BOM)
**Project:** Open-Cycle Liquid Bipropellant Rocket Engine Prototype

This document lists all off-the-shelf (COTS) components, sensors, and hardware required for the engine assembly and the static test stand. Custom machined parts (combustion chamber, injector) are not included here; refer to the `/CAD` directory for their specifications.
